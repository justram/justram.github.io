# Professional English is not perfect English

Opening beat:

"Today is not about sounding native. It is about making people think: this helps."

Frame the talk for NYCU STEM students:

- Professional English is not mainly grammar performance.
- It is a tool for helping real readers decide what counts as useful knowledge.
- The audience should feel relieved: the goal is not perfect English; the goal is reader-useful English.
